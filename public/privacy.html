PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated April 04, 2026

This Privacy Notice for Dreyco Pty Ltd (doing business as Axis) describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share your personal information when you use our services, including when you visit tryaxis.app or download and use our mobile application (Axis).

Questions or concerns? Contact us at dev@tryaxis.app

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect names, email addresses, and usernames you provide directly. We also collect calendar data and email data via Google OAuth with your permission. We collect device identifiers for push notifications, usage data, voice input when you use the microphone, and location data when relevant to the service.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

To deliver the Axis service, send push notifications and personalised briefings, respond to your inquiries, and send administrative information. We do not use your data for advertising.

DO WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION?

We share data only with service providers who help us operate Axis: Anthropic (AI processing), Google (OAuth, Calendar, Gmail), Stripe (payments), Clerk (authentication), Vercel (hosting), and Railway (backend infrastructure). We never sell your personal information.

HOW DO WE USE AI?

Axis uses AI services including Anthropic Claude, Perplexity, and Google Cloud AI to process your data and generate personalised responses. Your data is handled in accordance with each provider's data processing agreements.

HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR DATA?

We keep your personal information for as long as you have an account with us. You can request deletion at any time by emailing dev@tryaxis.app.

YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at dev@tryaxis.app.

GDPR (EU/UK)

If you are located in the EU or UK, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. We rely on consent and legitimate interest as our legal bases for processing.

AUSTRALIAN PRIVACY ACT

We comply with Australia's Privacy Act 1988. If you believe we have breached the Australian Privacy Principles, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner.

COOKIES

We use functional cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.
